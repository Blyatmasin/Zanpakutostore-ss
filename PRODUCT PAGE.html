KATEN KYOKOTSU (¥100.000)

A unique Zanpakuto that exists as a daisho sword pair, consisting of a tachi and a wakizashi. Their cross guards are rectangles, with the corners cut off, decorated by a pattern of cherry petals and lines, and each sports a dark-blue handle. Unlike other Zanpakuto, if Katen Kyokotsu is not in the mood for battle, it won't.

Shikai: Hana kaze midarete, kashin naki, tenpū midarete, tenma warau

Abilities: Takaoni, Kageoni, Irooni, Bushogoma, Daruma-san ga Koronda, Kageokuri.

Bankai: Katen Kyōkotsu: Karamatsu Shinjū

Abilities: Ichidanme: Tameraikizu no Wakachiai, Nidanme: Zanki no Shitone, Sandanme: Dangyo no Fuchi, Shime no Dan: Itokiribasami Chizome no Nodobue
